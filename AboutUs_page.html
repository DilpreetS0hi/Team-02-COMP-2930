Our Team Leaders

NO FOOD WASTAGE is a creative idea by BCIT CST students to deal with the problem of Food Wastage. A classroom project created with the aim to grab the opportunity at the ECOCITY WORLD SUMMIT 2019 in Vancouver.


“ If you waste a part of your groceries beacause they got expired and you literally forget about that OR, the issue is of how to use the expired items so that you need not throw them out. Having extra food, want to utilise it properly, NO FOOD WASTAGE app can help you out with these issues, Get the expiry tracker setup, check the suggestion, and donate the extra food to needy people. ”



Our mission is to reduce the food waste and utilize it to end food hunger. Go with the"NoFoodWastage" app, a step towards a better prosperous future.
